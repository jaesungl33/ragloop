# Refund Policy

Northstar Outdoors wants you to shop with confidence. If an item does not meet your expectations, you may request a refund within **30 days** of the delivery date shown on your order confirmation.

## Eligible items

Refunds apply to unused products in original packaging with tags attached. Final-sale and custom-engraved items are not refundable unless they arrive damaged or defective.

## How to start a return

Email support@northstar-outdoors.example with your order number and reason for the return. We will email a prepaid return label for domestic orders over $25. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the return.
