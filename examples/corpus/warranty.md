# Product Warranty

Northstar Outdoors backs the quality of our gear with a limited warranty against manufacturing defects.

## Coverage period

Most products carry a **one-year limited warranty** from the date of delivery. Tents and backpacks include a two-year warranty. Normal wear, misuse, and damage from accidents are not covered.

## How to file a claim

Contact support with photos of the defect and your order number. If approved, we will repair, replace, or refund the item at our discretion. You do not need to return the product until we send instructions.

## Exclusions

Batteries, consumables, and items marked “as-is” in clearance sales are excluded unless otherwise stated on the product page.
