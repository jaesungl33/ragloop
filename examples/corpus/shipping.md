# Shipping Policy

Northstar Outdoors ships to all 50 U.S. states. We do not ship internationally at this time.

## Rates and timing

Standard shipping (3–5 business days) is **free on orders over $50**. Orders under $50 incur a flat $6.95 shipping fee. Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available for $14.95 regardless of order total.

## Processing

Orders placed before 2:00 p.m. Eastern Time on business days ship the same day. Orders placed after the cutoff or on weekends ship the next business day. You will receive a tracking email when your package leaves our warehouse in Portland, Maine.
